About

I am a DPhil candidate in Economics at the University of Oxford, supervised by Howard Smith. My research is in industrial organisation — competition, market power and firm dynamics — and my doctoral work studies competition and procurement in public-sector cloud computing, centred on the UK government's G-Cloud framework.

Alongside the DPhil, I work as an economist in the Microeconomics Unit at the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA), contributing to its research programme on the state of competition, industrial strategy and corporate ownership.
